Welcome to the Sunday Morning Coffee Tour suggestion page! Please submit a coffee shop that you'd like to visit as part of the 2026 summer coffe tour.

What's the coffee tour? In Spring 2026, I will publish the tour list of SLC coffee shops and which Sunday I'll be there. Each Sunday, I'll bike to a different coffee shop in Salt Lake City, arriving at 9:30AM (weather permitting). I hope you'll join me!.

Why's coffee tour? I am seeking our the best coffee in SLC and I think it'd be fun to do with friends.
